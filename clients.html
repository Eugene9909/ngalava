About Us

Ngalava Solutions (Pty) Ltd is a 100% black female-owned company founded in 2018 by Khethiwe Ronica Hlungwana, a former nurse turned entrepreneur. Built on resilience and a vision for transformation, the company offers a dynamic blend of construction and logistics services across South Africa.

With a strong emphasis on innovation, safety, and client satisfaction, Ngalava Solutions has earned a reputation as a trusted partner in both sectors. Our goal is to bridge service gaps with efficiency, empowering clients through tailored logistics and infrastructure solutions.

Our Vision

To be a leading and trusted partner in delivering innovative construction and logistics solutions across Southern Africa.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality construction services and efficient logistics solutions that exceed client expectations through safety, reliability, and professionalism.
